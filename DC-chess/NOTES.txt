Run sequence:
1) Place Roboflow export in data/roboflow_yolov8/ (data.yaml + images)
2) Train detector: bash detection/train_detector.sh
3) After detector best.pt ready, run detection/detect_and_crop.py to crop pieces from boards
4) Prepare tiles (data/tiles_labeled/<class_name>/) and train classifier: python classifier/train_classifier.py
5) Preprocess full boards: warp + split tiles (use preprocessing/utils.py) then run inference/full_pipeline.py
